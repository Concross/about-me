  • Bus Mall

    This may just look like a simple shopping cart web app for ground transport's answer to Sky Mall, and it is! The idea behind this project was to create a simple site for market research where focus groups could vote for which products they would like to see in a Bus Mall magazine when they travel the roads in public transit. Take a look and vote for you favorites!

  • Typing++

    If you write code for a living and feel like your fingers fumble around for those clumsy syntax keys then you'll want to check this project out. Our Apprenti cohort came together to produce this beauty in 3 short days, all in vanilla JavaScript. The biggest takeway from developing this site was learning how to work as a team. We hit some bumps early but chose to adapt and overcome. At the end, our team was a well-oiled machine in a very brief amount of time. Huge accomplishment!
